# Security Policy

## Reporting a Vulnerability
If you discover a vulnerability in this project, we ask that you notify us as soon as possible. Please follow these steps to ensure prompt action:

1. **Contact**: Make sure to send a detailed email to [security@yourdomain.com](mailto:security@yourdomain.com) describing the vulnerability you discovered.
2. **Details**: Include as much information as possible, including a detailed description, steps to reproduce the issue, and any potential impacts.
3. **Disclosed**: Please refrain from disclosing the vulnerability publicly until we have had a chance to respond and address the issue.

## Response
We will respond to your report as quickly as possible, generally within 48 hours, and will keep you informed about the status of your report.
